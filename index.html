Short-form publishing utility

Create, review, and publish short-form videos.

What it does

Shortsmith helps turn long-form video into vertical clips and prepare publishing metadata for supported platforms.

Edit

Generate vertical clips from long-form videos and transcripts.

Review

Prepare captions, summaries, hashtags, titles, and timestamps before publishing.

Publish

Connect official platform APIs for authorized user publishing workflows.

Amaliometria Shortsmith is a local publishing tool maintained by Amalio Gomez Lopez.
